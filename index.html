Roughly 195 000 street art related crimes was reported in 2015,
an increase of 29 percent from the year before.
Graffiti towards the local traffic stands for the majority.
The number of crimes of this kind has varierd through 2006-2014,
but in 2015 there was clearly a bursting development.
In Sigtuna, where they raised a legal wall to paint, the number of reports
are on the other hand steadily decreasing. The reduction in 2014 was 72%.
