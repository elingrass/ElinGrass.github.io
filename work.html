I wanted to investigate whether people choose to call themselves feminists or not.
The target group was my facebook friends.
For my research, I created a form which 200 of 636 friends answered.
I had my thesis proved. A major part (70% to be precise) of my facebook friends
identifies themselves as a feminists.


However, during the research I found something more interesting.
I suddenly recieved some questions through messenger from people.
People (in specific: men) started to ask me about the actual requirements
to devote themselves to the frightening concept of being a feminist.
The truth is - It's not complicated at all.
As a result of this situation I had a flash of genius! Do you want to find out
if you're a feminist? Just take on this quiz.
